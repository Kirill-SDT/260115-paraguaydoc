# Designer Brief: ParaguayDoc Landing Page

**Project:** ParaguayDoc Immigration Services
**Date:** 2026-01-15
**For:** Designer Subagent
**From:** Analyst Subagent

---

## Overview

You're designing a landing page for an immigration services company helping Russian-speaking families and entrepreneurs relocate to Paraguay. The fundamental challenge is **building trust in a category plagued by scams**. Your design must feel human, transparent, and professional — never generic or template-like.

---

## Emotional Target

**Feeling to Evoke:** "Finally, someone I can trust. These are real people who understand my fears and will guide me through this."

**Not:** Corporate sterility, aggressive sales pressure, or generic "AI slop" aesthetics.

**Tone:** Calm confidence + human warmth. Think: **knowledgeable friend-lawyer**, not faceless agency.

---

## Structural Alternatives (Choose One or Hybridize)

### **Alternative 1: Story-Driven Journey**

**Concept:** Structure the page as a narrative arc showing emotional transformation.

**Visual Flow:**
```
[HERO: Full-bleed image — family looking contemplative, overlaid text]
"Вы решили переехать в Парагвай. Но вопросы не дают спать спокойно."

↓

[SECTION: The Guide]
Split layout: Photo of ParaguayDoc team (left), personal message (right)
"Мы — ваши соседи в Энкарнасьон, а не безликое агентство."

↓

[SECTION: The Path]
Visual timeline (horizontal scroll or vertical parallax)
Each milestone has: icon, description, timeframe, cost

↓

[SECTION: The Transformation]
Client testimonials with before/after framing:
"From uncertainty → To settled life in Paraguay"

↓

[CTA: Soft Invitation]
"Начните ваш путь с бесплатной консультации"
```

**Design Principles for This Approach:**
- Use large, emotive photography (if available) or atmospheric illustrations
- Typography should feel editorial: large serif headlines, clean sans-serif body
- Generous whitespace to create calm, not urgency
- Color palette: warm, earthy tones (terracotta, sage green, cream) to evoke Paraguay's landscape

**Pros:** Emotionally resonant, builds empathy before selling
**Cons:** Could feel too soft for business-oriented Segment B

---

### **Alternative 2: Fear-First / Transparency-Led**

**Concept:** Address the elephant in the room immediately. Lead with radical transparency.

**Visual Flow:**
```
[HERO: Bold, Direct]
Headline in extra-large type: "Боитесь, что вас обманут?"
Subhead: "Давайте говорить честно. Вот как это работает на самом деле."
[VIDEO EMBED: 60-second message from founder]

↓

[SECTION: The Real Process]
Expandable accordion or tabbed interface showing:
- Government fees (transparent breakdown)
- Our service fees (what's included)
- Timeline (best case / realistic case)
- What could go wrong (and how we handle it)

↓

[SECTION: Who We Are]
Grid layout: Team photos with names, roles, LinkedIn links
Office photo from Encarnación with map pin
Credentials/certifications displayed prominently

↓

[SECTION: Service Tiers]
Cards with brutally honest descriptions:
"Standard: Slower but affordable"
"Accelerated: Worth it if time matters"
"SUACE: For serious investors only"

↓

[CTA: Low-Pressure]
"Запишитесь на честный разговор (30 минут, бесплатно)"
```

**Design Principles for This Approach:**
- Typography: strong, confident sans-serif (but NOT Inter/Roboto) — consider Clash Display or Cabinet Grotesk
- Layout: asymmetric, editorial-inspired (Bloomberg, Guardian style)
- Color palette: high contrast for clarity — dark charcoal + bright accent (e.g., electric blue or warm amber)
- Micro-interactions: expand/collapse sections, smooth scrolling to create sense of control

**Pros:** Directly confronts skepticism, establishes trust through openness
**Cons:** Requires perfect tone calibration to avoid feeling defensive

---

### **Alternative 3: Timeline-First / Process Visualization**

**Concept:** Lead with an interactive timeline that demystifies the entire journey.

**Visual Flow:**
```
[HERO: Interactive Timeline]
Full-width, horizontally scrollable timeline as primary navigation:
[Документы] → [Прилет] → [Подача] → [Ожидание] → [Cédula]

Each stage clickable/tappable:
- Icon representation
- Time estimate
- Cost breakdown
- Client photo/testimonial from that stage

Toggle: [Standard Path] vs [Accelerated Path]

↓

[SECTION: Path Comparison]
Side-by-side visual comparison (not a boring table):
Timeline graphics showing Standard (6-7 mo) vs Accelerated (2.5-4 mo) vs SUACE (custom)
Each path has: duration bar, cost, # of visits required

↓

[SECTION: Real Example]
Case study laid out like a magazine article:
"Семья Ивановых: от первого звонка до Cédula"
Dates, milestones, photos, outcome

↓

[SECTION: Your Timeline Starts Here]
Form with visual progress indicator:
"Шаг 1 из 3: Расскажите о вашей ситуации"
```

**Design Principles for This Approach:**
- Typography: mix of technical monospace (for dates/times) + friendly sans-serif (for human content) — consider JetBrains Mono + Source Sans 3
- Layout: content-first, minimal decoration. Let the timeline graphic be the hero
- Color palette: Clean, modern — perhaps inspired by tech/SaaS (but warmer): slate + coral accent, or navy + gold
- Animation: smooth horizontal scroll, staggered reveals as user progresses through timeline

**Pros:** Reduces anxiety by making the unknown known. Appeals to both segments (families appreciate clarity, entrepreneurs appreciate efficiency)
**Cons:** Requires strong UX/visual design to avoid feeling sterile or overwhelming

## RECOMMENDED APPROACH: Hybrid Structure

**Combine the best of all three:**

1. **Hero Section (from Alt 2):** Open with fear acknowledgment + video from founder
   - Headline: "Переезд в Парагвай: без обмана, без бюрократии"
   - Subhead: "Мы живем в Энкарнасьон и честно расскажем, как это работает"
   - [60-sec video: founder's face, calm tone, office visible in background]

2. **Core Content (from Alt 3):** Interactive timeline as primary navigation/content device
   - Visual timeline showing Standard vs Accelerated paths
   - Each stage expandable with details, costs, testimonials

3. **Trust Layer (from Alt 1 + 2):** Intersperse human elements throughout
   - Team photos and bios
   - Client transformation stories
   - Office/location proof

4. **Service Tiers Section:** Clear, honest comparison (not a boring table — use visual cards with honest trade-offs)

5. **CTA:** Soft, consultation-focused (not "BUY NOW")
   - "Бесплатная 30-минутная консультация: разберем вашу ситуацию"

## Visual Mood & Inspiration

### What to Study (References):
1. **Editorial Clarity:** Bloomberg.com, The Guardian longreads — asymmetric layouts, generous whitespace, bold typography
2. **Transparent Tech:** Stripe.com, Linear.app — clean, modern, confident without being cold
3. **Human Services:** Better.com, Gusto.com — warm color palettes, real photos, approachable tone

### What to Avoid:
- Generic immigration agency sites with stock photos of happy families looking at passports
- Purple gradients on white backgrounds (cliché "AI slop")
- Symmetrical, centered layouts (feels templated)
- Inter or Roboto fonts (overused, lacks personality)

---

## Typography Recommendations

**Primary (Headings):**
- **Option A (Editorial Warmth):** Crimson Pro (serif) — conveys trustworthiness, established presence
- **Option B (Modern Confidence):** Clash Display or Cabinet Grotesk (geometric sans) — clean, distinctive
- **Option C (Technical Transparency):** IBM Plex Sans (corporate but not generic) — professional, approachable

**Secondary (Body Text):**
- Source Sans 3 or Noto Sans (excellent Cyrillic support)
- OR JetBrains Mono for timeline/data elements (adds technical credibility)

**Critical:** Choose fonts with robust Cyrillic character sets. Test with Russian text early.

**Font Pairing Example:**
- **Crimson Pro Bold** (48-72px) for headlines → conveys authority
- **Source Sans 3 Regular** (16-18px) for body → readable, warm
- **JetBrains Mono** (14px) for timeline labels, costs → adds precision

## Layout & Composition Principles

1. **Avoid Symmetry:** Use asymmetric layouts (e.g., 60/40 split, staggered content blocks) to create visual interest
2. **Generous Whitespace:** Don't fear empty space. It creates calm and focuses attention
3. **Content Hierarchy:** Use extreme size differences (3x+ jumps) between heading levels
4. **Grid System:** Establish a clear grid (12-column or 16-column) but break it intentionally for visual impact
5. **Scrolling Experience:** Design for vertical scroll with anchor navigation (not multiple pages). Consider parallax or reveal animations on scroll.

## Component Specifications

### Hero Section
- **Height:** Full viewport (100vh) on desktop, 80vh on mobile
- **Content:** Large headline (H1), supporting subhead, video embed (16:9 ratio, max-width 720px), subtle CTA button
- **Background:** Atmospheric gradient OR high-quality photo with overlay (NOT stock photo cliché)

### Timeline Component (Critical Element)
- **Style:** Horizontal scrollable on desktop, vertical stacked on mobile
- **Interaction:** Click/tap each stage to expand details inline
- **Visual Treatment:** Icons + connecting line + time labels
- **States:** Default, Hovered, Active/Expanded

### Service Tier Cards
- **Layout:** 3 cards side-by-side on desktop, stacked on mobile
- **Content per Card:**
  - Name (e.g., "Базовый", "Ускоренный", "SUACE")
  - Price (large, prominent)
  - Timeline estimate
  - 4-5 bullet points (benefits)
  - "Для кого" (who this is for)
  - Honest disclaimer ("Лучше выбрать Standard, если бюджет ограничен")
  - CTA button (consistent across all cards)
- **Visual Treatment:** Subtle border (not heavy drop shadow), hover state with slight lift

### Testimonial/Case Study Block
- **Layout:** Photo (left), quote + story (right)
- **Content:** Full name, photo, city, brief outcome summary
- **Visual Treatment:** Large pull quote in distinctive font, photo should be real (not stock)

### CTA / Form Section
- **Placement:** After service tiers and testimonials (not intrusive pop-up)
- **Fields:**
  - Name (text input)
  - Telegram handle (text input, placeholder: "@username")
  - Email (text input, optional)
  - Service interest (radio buttons: Standard / Accelerated / SUACE)
  - Message (textarea, optional)
- **Button:** Large, clear, action-oriented: "Записаться на консультацию"
- **Visual Treatment:** Form should feel approachable, not intimidating. Consider inline validation with friendly messaging.

## Mobile-First Considerations

**70%+ of traffic expected to be mobile.** Design mobile layout first, then enhance for desktop.

**Mobile Priorities:**
1. **Fast Load:** Hero image optimized, video lazy-loaded
2. **Thumb-Friendly:** All interactive elements minimum 44x44px
3. **Readable Text:** Body text minimum 16px, line-height 1.5
4. **Simplified Navigation:** Hamburger menu acceptable, but anchor links preferred
5. **Timeline:** Convert horizontal timeline to vertical stacked cards on mobile

**Breakpoints:**
- Mobile: 320px - 767px
- Tablet: 768px - 1023px
- Desktop: 1024px+

## Trust-Building Visual Elements

1. **Team Photos:** Real headshots (not stock). Show faces, names, roles. If possible, photo of team together in office.
2. **Office/Location Proof:** Photo of Encarnación office exterior or interior. Include map with pin showing location.
3. **Video Message:** Founder speaking directly to camera (not overly produced, feels authentic)
4. **Client Testimonials:** Real photos + full names (no anonymous quotes)
5. **Process Documentation:** Redacted screenshots of actual documents (Admisión Temporaria approval, Cédula, etc.) to prove legitimacy
6. **Telegram Channel Embed:** If possible, show recent posts/updates from @Paraguaydoc channel
7. **Credentials:** Display any licenses, certifications, or official registrations prominently

## Accessibility & Performance

- **Color Contrast:** Must meet WCAG 2.1 AA (4.5:1 for body text, 3:1 for large text)
- **Focus States:** Clear, visible focus indicators for all interactive elements (keyboard navigation)
- **Alt Text:** All images must have meaningful alt text, especially team photos and testimonials
- **Font Loading:** Use `font-display: swap` to prevent invisible text during font load
- **Image Optimization:** All images compressed, use WebP with fallbacks, lazy-load below-the-fold content

---

## Content Tone & Voice (For Designer to Consider in Layout)

- **Formal but Friendly:** Use "Вы" (formal you), but write like a human, not a lawyer
- **Transparent:** Acknowledge problems directly ("Базовый тариф занимает 6-7 месяцев — это долго, но честно")
- **Reassuring:** Emphasize guidance and support ("Мы будем с вами на каждом этапе")
- **Specific:** Use exact numbers, not ranges ("$1,400" not "от $1,000")
